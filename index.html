Personal Training Manual

The Aesthetic Blueprint

Broad shoulders. Full chest. Tight waist.

Your four-day blueprint for the V-taper.

180 CM63 KGBEGINNERGOAL · V-TAPERSUN–WED

Principles

Every session builds one shape — the V-taper. Master each movement before chasing weight, add a rep or a little load over time (progressive overload), and protect your knees with slow tempo and joint-friendly machines on leg day.

Fuel

Eat in a surplus

~300–500 kcal above maintenance. Protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg (≈100–140 g). At 63 kg, eating consistently grows you as much as training.

Warm-Up

5–10 minutes

Light cardio, then 1–2 lighter ramp-up sets on your first lift. Prime the joints — don't tire them out.

Rest

90–120 seconds

120s on big compounds, 90s on isolation. Rest long enough to hit your reps with clean form.

Tempo

2–3s lowering

Control every eccentric (the "ecc" in each exercise). Lower slow, lift with intent — growth and joint safety both live here.
